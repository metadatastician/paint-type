## Standing / continuous items

These are **not** one-off make-great tasks — they are continuous obligations addressed
"as they arise" each session, with **no per-occurrence board churn**. The underlying
items (scanner alerts, wiki gaps) auto-add/remove at source; the board carries **one
durable `STAND-NN` item** per obligation that points at that source. This is the answer
to "do we add/subtract as they occur?": no — one standing item, a recurring triage.
